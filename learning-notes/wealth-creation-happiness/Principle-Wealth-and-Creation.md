## Three Important Equilibriums

 - Debt growth is ***in line*** with the income growth that is required to service debts
 - Economic capacity utilisation is neither ***too high nor too low***
 - Projected returns of **equities**(stock) are above the projected returns of **bonds** which are above the projected returns of **cash** by appropriate risk premiums - ***cash < bonds < equities***
## Two Levers

- Monetary Policy - brakes/gas put on - tight/lose
- Fiscal Policy 
Economic and market movements are like perpetual motion machine of the interactions of these.
