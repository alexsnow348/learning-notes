# 🇩🇪 German B1 Study Plan: Month 1 (March 2026)

**Goal:** Transition from A2 to B1. Focus on **Subordinate Clauses** and **Professional Life**.

## 📅 Weekly Schedule Breakdown

### **Week 1: The Job Search**

- **Main Course:** [Nicos Weg B1](https://learngerman.dw.com/de/top-thema/s-55861562) - Units 1 to 4.
- **Grammar:** Review _Dass-Sätze_ (Subordinate clauses starting with "that").
- **Writing Task:** Draft a formal email asking for an internship (50-70 words).
- **Vocabulary:** Focus on job titles and workplace equipment.
### **Week 2: The Workplace**

- **Main Course:** Nicos Weg B1 - Units 5 to 8.
- **Grammar:** Master _Weil-Sätze_ (Moving the verb to the end with "because").
- **Writing Task:** Forum post: "Is a high salary more important than free time?" (80 words).
- **Listening:** Watch [Logo! News](https://www.zdf.de/kinder/logo) /[Goethe- Prüfung B1](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLLI7uRTZ_9iklsHkHO8ubLscy_SyTxxv-&si=DwfzBfdUc3ijQvWd) twice this week.

### **Week 3: Daily Life & Conflict**

- **Main Course:** Nicos Weg B1 - Units 9 to 12.
- **Grammar:** Practice _Obwohl_ (although) and _Wenn_ (if/when).
- **Speaking Task:** Record a 1-minute voice note describing your current living situation.
- **Vocabulary:** Household items, apartment searching, and common arguments.

### **Week 4: Health & Wellness**

- **Main Course:** Nicos Weg B1 - Units 13 to 16.
- **Grammar:** Review _Modalverben_ (should, must, can) in the _Präteritum_ (past tense).
- **Speaking Task:** Comparison: How do people eat in your country vs. Germany?
- **Review:** Go through all new words from the month in your Flashcard app.
## 🏗️ Academic Writing Template: Formal Email

_Use this for your Week 1 task. For academic/professional purposes, precision is key._

> **Betreff:** Anfrage bezüglich eines Praktikumsplatzes
> 
> **Sehr geehrte Damen und Herren,**
> 
> mein Name ist [Your Name] und ich interessiere mich sehr für Ihr Unternehmen. Da ich ab nächstem Jahr in Deutschland studieren möchte, suche ich ein Praktikum im Bereich [Subject].
> 
> Ich lerne zurzeit Deutsch auf dem Niveau B1. Ich freue mich über eine positive Rückmeldung.
> 
> **Mit freundlichen Grüßen,**
> 
> [Your Full Name]
