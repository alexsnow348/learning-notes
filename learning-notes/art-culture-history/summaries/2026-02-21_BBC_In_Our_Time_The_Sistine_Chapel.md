### The Sistine Chapel

**Topic:** The Sistine Chapel
**Host:** Melvyn Bragg
**Broadcast Date:** March 31, 2022
**Summary:** This episode delves into the monumental work of Michelangelo in the Vatican's Sistine Chapel. The discussion covers his creation of the Genesis frescoes on the ceiling and The Last Judgement on the altar wall. It highlights how Michelangelo's achievement was a powerful affirmation of the spiritual and political standing of the Vatican, Rome, and the Catholic Church. The episode also touches upon the personal challenge it represented for Michelangelo, already renowned for his sculpture of David, showcasing his mastery of human form and the astounding potential of art. The discussion features insights from art historians Catherine Fletcher, Sarah Vowles, and Matthias Wivel.
