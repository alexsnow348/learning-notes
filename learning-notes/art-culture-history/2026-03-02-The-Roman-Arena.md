## Analysis
The core takeaway is that venues served as catalysts for social ordering and collective memory. They reveal how spectatorship reinforced status and political power, while also offering a space where shared cultural narratives could be rehearsed and challenged.

## Reflection
What parallels do you see between ancient Roman venues and modern public spaces (stadiums, theatres, malls)? How does the design of a space shape social dynamics and discourse?
